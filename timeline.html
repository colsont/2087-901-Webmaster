    Company History Staff and Members

June 9th, 2014 - Company Startup

On this momentous day, our founders came together and came up with an idea for the future of the health industry: Eve Telemedicine.

May 16th, 2016 - The First Day

After 2 long years of work and planning, Eve Telemedicine was launched and had its first patients.

November 23rd, 2017 - Going Public

In order to attract more investors, we decided that it was best to open investing to the public with our very own stock.

February 8nd, 2018 - Product Expansion

In order to expand, the board of directors decided to announce medicine to be delivered to home and vaccines to be administered at our center

October 21st, 2019 - Opening New Centers

In order to make our vaccine administrations more accessible, we launched centers across the country.

March 15, 2020 - Lockdown

With the emergence of COVID-19 and a world-wide lockdown, our team got to work to help the community in as many ways as possible.
