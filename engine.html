Engine






The Engine

Designed for performance

Evo Inc. has a beautiful patent-pending design on their electric engine.

The Evo engine is an all electric motor that can be built to vary between 200 and 500 kW (268hp to 670hp).

This very energy efficient motor can also be paired with up to 4 total engines, adding a theoretical max power of 2000 kW (2682hp). This makes the Evo engine the most powerful electrical motor available on the market. The Evo engine is put in every current Evo Inc. Model.
