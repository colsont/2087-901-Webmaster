Model III

Evo Model III

Designed for you

The Model III is Evo Inc.'s best car yet, by far.

The Model III is a powerhouse. With over 500hp going to the front and rear wheels, it is the fastest electric car available on the market.

The range has once again increased, from 290 miles to 350 miles on a full charge. The Model III is designed as a sedan, similar to the Model I, and unlike the Model II.
