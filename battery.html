Battery






The Battery

Designed for efficiency

All the Evo Inc. Models use the new lithium ceramic batteries (LCBs) these batteries can provide a high voltage while being almost completely puncture-proof.

These lithium ceramic batteries are flexible, thin, and very energy efficient. This makes the batteries extremely safe.

The batteries are rated at 5kW per cell, and since the batteries are almost paper thin, many can be easily stacked while maintaining a very low weight.
